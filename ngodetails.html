All About Pasaydan



Pasaydan Seva Prakalp is a NGO and service project started in the year 2009 and has turned into a banyan tree since then. Pasaydan means gift of divine grace and it also is a universal prayer for the welfare of human beings. This NGO works for the children of farmers who committed suicide in Marathwada, the children of sugarcane workers, the children of those who earn their living by collecting garbage and scraps in the city, and the destitute children who are underprivileged.

The children with normal family background grow in an healthy surrounding. There is vast inequality amongst them and lost, homeless children. This is where PASAYDAN comes into the picture. Their job is to plug the gaps left in their social and educational status by improving their social and educational skills.PASAYDAN undertakes a variety of activities, most of which are aimed at improving the social-cultural status of children with limited means. From providing direct benefit (like distributing nutrition feed to malnourished children) to enabling and empowering people (like making our children realise the importance of their education at school), the work of PASAYDAN has a far-reaching impact in helping underprivileged and deprived children march ahead in life.
