About Us

Previous Next

I am Govardhan Darade from a small village Khadki, Devla in the hills of Balaghat in Beed district. Social work is neither inherited nor acquired, it is only born out of commitment to society, passion,self experience and surrounding social problems. For the children of farmers who committed suicide in Marathwada, the children of sugarcane workers, the children of those who earn their living by colllecting garbage and scraps in the city, and the destitute children who are underprivileged, this service project started in the year 2009 has turned into a banyan tree since then.
